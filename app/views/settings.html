Flickr Downloadr 3 (v3.0.0)

Flickr Downloadr is an app that enables you to find, pick and download photos from the Flickr.com service.

 

Focus for the app have always been on simplicity and elegance. The main features needed, in the simplest form.

 

This application is a third party application and is not association with Flickr or Yahoo in any way or form.
