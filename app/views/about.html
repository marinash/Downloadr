Flickr Downloadr

Release v

Flickr Downloadr is an app that enables you to find and pick photos to download from the Flickr.com service.

 

Focus for the app have always been on simplicity and elegance. The main features needed, in the simplest form. The software and service is hosted and provided by brain.no

Photo Licenses

You are allowed to search and view photos with any licenses, but only those with Creative Commons license is allowed to be downloaded to your local computer.

The default selection will only show photos that can be downloaded, but in the bottom options you can enable showing search results of photos that are not downloadable. You can right-click on those photos, to take you directly to the Flickr page where you can send a message to the user, asking them to change permissions.

This is useful as many using Flickr is doing it with the intention of sharing memories, but doesn't necessary understand all the licensing issues.
